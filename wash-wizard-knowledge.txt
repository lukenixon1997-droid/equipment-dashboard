Equipment: H25 Hydra-Cell Pump
Topic: Priming / Removing Air From Oil Diaphragm

Problem:
Hydra-Cell pump shakes or vibrates aggressively because air is trapped in the oil diaphragm. This can happen randomly, after installing a new pump, or between oil changes.

Fix: Free air from oil diaphragm without disassembling pump
1. Turn off pump and lock it out.
2. Remove inlet and outlet piping from pump.
3. Install the correct plug in the pump outlet.
4. Install air chuck fitting in pump inlet.
5. Connect airline to regulator and set regulator to 10 PSI.
6. Attach regulator to air chuck to pressurize pump diaphragm.
7. Open oil fill port.
8. Rotate pump pulley until zero air bubbles come from oil fill port.
9. Reassemble inlet/outlet piping.
10. Verify pump runs smoothly.

Safety:
- Lockout pump before removing piping.
- Use only low regulated air pressure: 10 PSI.
- Do not run pump dry.
- Do not work on pressurized piping while pump is operating.

Equipment: Velocity Water Softener
Topic: Temporary Single Tank Operation

Purpose:
Operations-level procedure to keep water online temporarily when one Velocity VS2 softener valve fails. Intended for short-term operation only: 1–2 days. Avoids advanced settings and wiring changes.

Step 1: Identify Failed Valve
- Identify whether Valve A or Valve B failed.
- Valve A: controller has four wires connected to motherboard.
- Valve B: controller has two wires connected to motherboard.
- Do not remove wiring.
- Do not open controller housing.

Step 2: Power Down Failed Softener
1. Unplug power to the FAILED softener.
2. Check display on GOOD softener.

If GOOD softener display shows Standby:
1. Press and hold NEXT + REGEN together for 3 seconds.
2. Valve will reset and enter Softening mode.

If GOOD softener display shows Softening:
- No action required.
- Proceed to Step 3.

Do not enter menus.
Do not change settings.

Step 3: Isolate Failed Softener
1. Turn OFF incoming water supply to FAILED softener.
2. GOOD softener will continue supplying water while failed softener remains isolated.

Important Temporary Operation Notice:
- While one softener is offline, system will not regenerate normally.
- GOOD softener may display an error once treatment capacity is reached.
- This is acceptable short-term only, 1–2 days.

Do Not:
- Do not change controller settings.
- Do not access advanced menus.
- Do not move wiring between valves.

After Repair:
1. Plug repaired unit back in.
2. Turn incoming water supply back ON.
3. Maintenance should verify normal duplex operation.
